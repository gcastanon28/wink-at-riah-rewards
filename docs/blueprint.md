# **App Name**: WinkLuxe Rewards

## Core Features:

- User Account & Dashboard: Secure client login and a personalized dashboard displaying current points, progress to the next reward tier, and quick navigation.
- Rewards Catalog: Browse a curated selection of featured and available rewards with clear point redemption values.
- Reward Redemption: Clients can easily redeem their accumulated points for desired rewards within the app.
- Visit History & Point Details: Access a comprehensive log of past appointments and a detailed breakdown of points earned and spent.
- Personalized Profile Management: Allow clients to update their personal information and communication preferences securely.
- Appointment Booking Call-to-Action: A prominent and accessible button linking directly to the appointment booking system.
- AI-Powered Personalized Tips: A tool that generates tailored lash care advice or product recommendations based on the client's service history or accumulated points.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, luxurious deep rose (#E246A9) for accent elements and interactive components.
- Background color: A subtle, dark charcoal with a hint of purple (#181517) to provide a rich, sophisticated base.
- Accent color: A soft, elegant lavender-pink (#D480CB) to highlight secondary information or states.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for modern readability.
- Utilize minimalist line icons that align with the beauty and lash theme, in monochromatic or primary accent colors.
- Prominent sidebar navigation for Dashboard, My Points, Rewards, Visit History, and Profile. Content sections displayed within cards featuring smooth rounded corners.
- Subtle and fluid transition animations for dashboard updates and card interactions, enhancing the premium user experience.
